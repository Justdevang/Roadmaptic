#Critical issues

Critical
Navigation cluttered with 10+ niche blog links
The footer/nav lists links like "Quantum-Safe Engineering", "BCI & Neuro-Interface Design", and "Decentralized Identity" — topics far outside a beginner's learning path. This confuses the audience and dilutes the core value prop.

Critical
"Initializing AI Curriculum Engine..." loader — does it actually do anything?
If this is just a cosmetic animation that never resolves to an interactive form or result, it actively hurts trust. Users will wait, realize nothing happened, and bounce. Either make it functional or remove it entirely.

Critical
No social proof, testimonials, or sample outputs
There's nothing to demonstrate what the roadmap actually looks like. Showing a sample AI-generated roadmap (e.g. "Week 1–12 Web Dev Path for a Beginner") would massively increase conversion.

#Moderate Issues

Headline is generic — "From Zero to Job-Ready" is everywhere
This tagline is used by dozens of bootcamps. Try something more specific and differentiating: "Your personalized 12-week coding plan, built in 30 seconds" — it's concrete, time-aware, and unique.

Moderate
The "Free Coding Bootcamp Generator" section is vague
It mentions Data Science, Web Dev, Python, ML — but doesn't show a form or next step. Users don't know how to actually get their roadmap. Add a multi-step form: pick your goal → your level → your hours/week → generate.

Moderate
Feature list (3 cards) lacks specifics
"Personalized AI Paths", "Curated Top Tutorials", "Schedule Optimized" are vague. Rewrite them with concrete hooks: "Your skills + target job → a week-by-week curriculum" tells users exactly what they'll get.

Moderate
No visible email capture or free account sign-up
If a user visits from a reel and leaves, you've lost them forever. Add a lightweight "Get your free roadmap via email" form — even just email + goal — to build a list.

Moderate
No FAQ or "How it works" section
Visitors from social media arrive skeptical. A 3-step "How it works" section (Tell us your goal → AI builds your plan → Follow it week by week) removes friction and answers the #1 question: "What do I actually get?"
