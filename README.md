## 🚀 What is Roadmaptic?

Roadmaptic turns the internet's infinite noise into a **clear, week-by-week learning curriculum** tailored to your goals, skills, and schedule.

Tell it:
- 🎯 Your **target role** (Frontend Dev, Data Scientist, ML Engineer, etc.)
- 📊 Your **current skill level**
- ⏱️ Your **weekly availability** (5 hrs or 40 hrs — it adapts)

Get back a **personalized 12-week roadmap** with curated links to the best free resources on the internet. No paid course upsells. No fluff.

**🔗 [Try it live → roadmaptic.qzz.io](https://roadmaptic.qzz.io)**

---

## 😤 The Problem We're Solving

Self-taught developers, data scientists, and designers waste **hundreds of hours** figuring out:
- What to learn
- In what order
- From which resource

The internet has infinite knowledge but zero structure. Most tutorials are built for the creator's convenience, not your career path.

Roadmaptic flips this — it **starts with where you want to end up** and builds the path backwards.

## 🛠️ How It Works

```
1. Enter your target role
        ↓
2. Enter your current skill level
        ↓
3. Enter your weekly study hours
        ↓
4. AI generates your 12-week roadmap
        ↓
5. Follow it week by week with curated resource links
```
